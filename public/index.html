About This Site

In the spring of 2003, Vianet Internet Solutions purchased Webhart, a Pembroke-based ISP company.

One of the goals of Vianet Internet Solutions was to set up a website where Webhart customers, Vianet customers, and the rest of the Ottawa Valley's surfing public could meet to gather information, to communicate and to share information on local events and issues. This site is the result.

Contents: Here you'll find lots of useful information and items, from our popular Site Of The Day feature to a community message board system, classified ads, local link listings and information on local events. Use our contact form to send us your comments and suggestions regarding this site.
