About This Site

In the spring of 2003, Vianet Inc. purchased Webhart, a Pembroke-based ISP company.

One of the goals of Vianet Inc. was to set up a website where Webhart customers, Vianet customers, and the rest of the Ottawa Valley's surfing public could meet to gather information, to communicate and to share information on local events and issues. This site is the result.

Contents: Here you'll find lots of useful information and items, from our popular local link listings and information on local events.
